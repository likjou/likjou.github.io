My Projects 😀

TBitTorrent

In my journey of exploring new technology, i was mesmerized by how BitTorrent works and how people can share large files without having to upload them to a server. At the same time, i always wanted to build an application or program that works exactly like QBitTorrent which is why TBitTorrent is created during my FYP :D. Entire application can be bundled into single binary and works on many platform with the power of GO programming language.

link to project

APResource

During the creation of TBitTorrent, i thought that a torrent client would not be useful if there were no torrent files to be downloaded. So, APResource was created as a resource sharing platform with the power of Django. Dr Octopus in Spider man 3 once said the power of Django in the palm of my hand. Just joking, he never said that. The design was inspired by Nyaa.si, an anime torrenting site. APResource is used in my FYP as a second program to support the TBitTorrent.
